The Challenge: Rethinking Study Habits

Many students spend hours rewriting notes, struggling to memorize facts, and using inefficient study methods. This creates wasted time, frustration, and poor learning outcomes.

  • ⏳ Time-consuming note rewriting
  • ❌ Weak active recall & retention
  • 📚 Limited access to effective study tools

Our Solution: AI Study Buddy

AI Study Buddy transforms student notes into interactive flashcards using Artificial Intelligence. By encouraging active recall, students study smarter and retain knowledge longer.
